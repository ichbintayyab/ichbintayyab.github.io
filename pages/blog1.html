My Journey Into Software Engineering

Software engineering has become an important part of my life. My journey into this field has been both simple and fascinating, driven by my curiosity for technology and passion for solving problems.

Learning Phase

I am currently pursuing a BS in Software Engineering at Lahore Garrison University. Over the past two semesters, I have learned the fundamentals of programming, logic building, and teamwork. These early experiences helped me understand how software development works in a practical setting.

Online Learning

In addition to university classes, I explored online platforms like Udemy, Coursera, and YouTube to accelerate my learning. These resources allowed me to gain hands-on skills, work on small projects, and understand real-world applications of programming.

Projects & Practice

To strengthen my knowledge, I started building small projects like calculators, to-do apps, and basic websites. These practical exercises helped me consolidate my learning, improve problem-solving skills, and understand the importance of clean, maintainable code.

Future Plan

My ultimate goal is to dive deep into Artificial Intelligence (AI) and understand how it works. I aim to apply my knowledge to build intelligent systems, and eventually expand my learning towards AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) research.

Conclusion

Software engineering has been an exciting journey so far, filled with learning, experimentation, and growth. I look forward to continuing this path, expanding my skills, and contributing to innovative projects in the future.
